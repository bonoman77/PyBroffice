Frequently Asked Questions
Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades are prorated, downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans. Payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What happens if I cancel?

Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation. You can export or reactivate during this period.

Do you offer nonprofit discounts?

Yes, we offer 50% off for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact sales to apply.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. No questions asked.

Need a custom solution?

Contact our sales team for custom pricing and features tailored to your organization.
